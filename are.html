FROTH FLOATATION PROCESS

FROTH FLOATATION PROCESS IS BASED ON THE
FACT THAT SULPHADE ORES ARE PREFERETAILLY WETTED BY PINE OIL,
WHEREAS THE GANGUE PARTICAL ARE WATTED WITH WATER.A ROTATING PUDDLE
AGITALES THE MIXTURE AND DERE AIR IN IT.AS A RESULT A FFP CARRYING THE
MINERALS PARICLES IS FORMED.THE FROTH BEING LIGHT COMES UPWARD AND SKIMMED
OFF.IT IS THEN DRIED FOR RECOVERING THR ORE PARTICLES .
